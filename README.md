# Zara Sales EDA

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes the **Zara Sales Dataset** using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to discover the main factors affecting sales volume.

The analysis focuses on:
- Price impact on sales
- Promotion effectiveness
- Seasonal sales trends

---


## 📊 Key Insights

### ✅ Promotion is the Biggest Sales Driver
Products on promotion sell about **61.9% more** than non-promoted products.

### ✅ Price Elasticity is High
There is a moderate negative correlation between price and sales:

\[
r = -0.34
\]

As prices increase, sales volume decreases.

### ✅ Seasonal Demand is Strong
- **Summer** and **Winter** have the highest sales.
- **Spring** and **Autumn** show lower performance.

---

## 💡 What We Learned
- Promotions significantly boost sales.
- Customers are sensitive to price changes.
- Sales are highly dependent on seasonality.
- Data analysis helps improve business decisions.

---

## 🚀 Recommendations
- Increase promotions during peak seasons.
- Use competitive pricing strategies.
- Prepare more inventory for Summer and Winter.
- Track promotion performance regularly.
